Unemployment Insights in Victoria

Employment Status by Gender and Education Level

The stacked bar chart shows employment status across different gender and education groups. Males with postgraduate degrees have the highest full-time employment rate of 77%, while females without a non-school qualification have a much lower full-time employment rate of 24%.

Unemployment Rate Over Time (Victoria)

The area chart below illustrates the unemployment rate trends in Victoria from 2018 to 2023. It captures key events like the sharp increase during the COVID-19 pandemic, which peaked in July 2020 at 7.5%, and the steady decline afterward, reaching as low as 3.5% by mid-2023.
